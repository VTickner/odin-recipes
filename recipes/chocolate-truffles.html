Chocolate Truffles Recipe

  • Home

Description

TThese simple, yet rich homemade sweets are every bit as delicious as truffles from a high-priced sweet store.

Ingredients

  • 540g chocolate chips
  • 1 can (397g) sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Coatings

  • Cocoa Powder
  • Icing sugar
  • Finely chopped toasted nuts
  • Flaked coconut
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. Melt chocolate chips with sweetened condensed milk in large saucepan over low heat.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  3. Pour into a medium bowl. Cover and chill for 2 to 3 hours or until firm.
  4. Shape into 1 inch (2.5cm) balls and then roll in desired coating.
  5. Chill for 1 hour or until firm.
