Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Description

These chocolate chocolate chip cookies are made with cocoa powder and chocolate chips to guarantee serious chocolate flavour in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 285g cups white granulated sugar
  • 226g butter, softened
  • 2 medium eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 280g plain flour
  • 80g cocoa powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 360g chocolate chips
  • 50g chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Beat sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla extract in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
  3. Combine flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt in another bowl; stir into butter mixture until well blended.
  4. Mix in chocolate chips and walnuts.
  5. >Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto a baking tray.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 8 to 10 minutes, or just until set.
  7. Cool slightly on the baking tray before transferring to wire racks to cool completely.
