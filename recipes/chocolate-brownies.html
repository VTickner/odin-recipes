Chocolate Brownies Recipe

Description

These cake-like brownies are the most delicious, fudgy treats that I have ever eaten. They are so moist and super quick and easy to make. For best results, use Dutch-processed cocoa. For less cake-like brownies, use all-purpose flour instead of self-rising.

Ingredients

  • 190g white granulated sugar
  • 113g butter, melted
  • 2 large eggs
  • 65g self-raising flour
  • 40g cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 50g chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease and flour an 8x8 or 9x9 inch baking tin.
  3. Beat together sugar and butter in a medium bowl. Add eggs and mix well.
  4. Combine flour, cocoa powder, and salt; stir into the sugar mixture.
  5. Mix in vanilla extract, then stir in walnuts.
  6. Spread evenly into the prepared tin.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes, until edges are firm.
  8. Cool before cutting into squares.
